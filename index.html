About

I build intelligent systems that see, think, and stay secure.

I'm a third-year Computer Engineering student at E-JUST (Egypt-Japan University of Science and Technology) specializing in AI/ML, Computer Vision, Cybersecurity, and Robotics. I build end-to-end systems that solve real-world problems—from malware detection with 97.3% accuracy to underwater ROV computer vision pipelines.

Currently, I'm wrapping up my graduation project—Dual-Frequency RFID Defense Architecture—a physical anti-cloning system combining HF + UHF chips with server-side collision detection and rolling tokens. I'm also a core member of RoboX, a robotics academy team focused on autonomous systems and AI integration.
